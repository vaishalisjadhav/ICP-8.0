Welcome to the Solapur Smart City


Here are some highlights of our City Smart-City:

1. Darmveer Sambhaji Lake

It is one of the largest lakes in Solapur that was recently sheltered by the government for
a beautification and conservation mission. Take a long walk along the lake in the evening and
spend some leisure hours watching people.It can be a good pit-stop for you
and your family to steal a few moments of self-indulgence before turning in for the night.

2. Science Centre


The Solapur Science Center is one of the most visited attractions in the city
and arguably the most iconic science museum in the state. The establishment of this
center was aimed to propagate and promote science among the masses.
Through its various exhibitions that can be participated in throughout the year,
it propagates scientific principles and ideas through simple visual showcases for kids and adults.

3. Siddheshwar Temple


This temple was built by yogi Shri Siddharameshwar to honor Lord Siddheshwar,
one of the many forms of Lord Vishnu and Shiva. He was a devoted follower of Sri Mallikajuna
of Srisailam and installed 68 shivlings all in the city of Solapur.
Visit this temple during sunset and you can catch a glimpse of its reflection
in the water that surrounds this dynamic structure.
